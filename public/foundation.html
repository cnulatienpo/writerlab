🧱 Foundation Room

This room teaches the foundational beats and forces behind every story. Learn these, and you can build anything.

🔥 Desire

Who wants what? Why can't they say it out loud? How does it drive the scene?

⚔️ Conflict

What gets in their way—inside or outside? What's the pressure point?

🔁 Change

What shifts? In the world, the character, the dynamic? Change is proof something mattered.

🎬 Scene

Where and when are we? What do we feel, smell, hear? Ground it in something real.

🚨 Climax

The moment of highest intensity or consequence—when everything breaks or breaks through.

🔄 Turning Point

Something irreversible happens. The story veers off its path. No going back.

♻️ Reversal

The power dynamic flips. The expected becomes unexpected. Who’s in charge now?

🧠 Decision

A character chooses—under pressure. Their choice reveals who they are (or want to be).

🎯 Outcome

What happens as a result of their decision? Did the story earn its end?

📉 Stakes

What’s on the line? Why does it matter? Stakes make readers care.

🎁 Setup / Payoff

What’s planted early that blooms later? How does the story reward attention?
